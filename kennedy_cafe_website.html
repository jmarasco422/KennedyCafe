Our Story

A True Small Town Gem

Kennedy Cafe is the kind of place that reminds you why small town diners are irreplaceable. A warm smile when you walk in, a fresh cup of coffee before you even ask, and a menu full of honest, homemade food made with care.

Whether you're a local stopping in before work or a traveler passing through on the way north, you'll find exactly what you were looking for here.
